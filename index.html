Our Work

--------- Hygge ---------
(hoo-guh)

If you are not familiar with the Danish word "hygge", let us fill you in on what it is, without a doubt, our favorite word.

There is not direct English translation for "hygge", but in a few words , it means to be comfortable, cozy, and content. We like to describe it as a perfect conditions that come naturally. Think of those special feelings that you only get from sitting around the campfire in the mountains on a clear night, pouring over a great book on an unexpected snow day, or dinner with friends that made for the perfect evening. It's the sense of everything feeling just right, and you are exactly where you want to be.

This is how we want you to feel working with Hygge House. With our level of expertise, creativity, flexibility, and positive attitude, and we bring to all of our projects all the necessary elements to create that hygge feeling.

Our recipe is simple, we create and deliver video concepts that have purpose and value. With our upfront and transparent approach to pricing, direction, and process, we can promise that we are not going to be like that random friend of a friend that shows up and make everything awkward.

We hope that you will feel hygge working with us, doing what we truly love.
